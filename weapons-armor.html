DragonDex:
Weapons and Armor Index

This index lists all weapons and items of armor (magical and non-magical) from all game systems that were detailed in the magazine. "Weapon" is defined as an item whose purpose is to cause hit-point loss to an opponent by piercing, slashing, bludgeoning, or burning. It does not include miscellaneous magic items that can cause harm magically or those which cause diseases, poisoning, curses, ability score loss, or other harm.

"Armor" includes full suits and pieces of any type of armor, including shields, helmets, gauntlets, and bracers. Not included are items that provide armor bonuses via magical effects only.
